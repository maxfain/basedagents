Under the hood: the BasedAgents registry

Keyring works because every key is bound to a cryptographic identity. Agents register in the public BasedAgents registry — an Ed25519 keypair, a proof-of-work stamp, a permanent ag_ ID no one can fake. You claim your agents with your email; your first approval mints a passkey that signs every grant. The result is a verifiable chain of authority from a human decision to every key an agent ever uses — not a database row that says "trust us."

The registry is open, platform-neutral, and free to build on.
